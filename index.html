About the novella

Necessary Cuts follows a boy growing up in the rural South. On a fish hatchery. In a farmhouse full of snakes. Across a series of small towns where nothing quite fits. His father collects animal bones and is slowly fading away. His mother holds everything together until she can't. And a skeleton raccoon finds him in the dark, carrying gifts no one asked for.

It's a book about the strange things that find you when the real ones won't stay. About what it costs to survive.

Necessary Cuts is the winner of the 2026 Miami University Press Novella Prize and will be published by Miami University Press.

About the author

Chris Dodds lives in Oklahoma City with his wife and two feral goblins. Necessary Cuts is his first book.
