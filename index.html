About the novella

Necessary Cuts follows a boy growing up in the rural South — on a fish hatchery above a lake, then a farmhouse full of snakes, then a series of small towns where nothing quite fits. His father collects animal bones and is slowly fading away. His mother holds everything together until she can't. And a skeleton raccoon keeps showing up at the worst possible moments, carrying gifts no one asked for.

It's a story about the places you're from and the ones that get taken from you. About watching a parent disappear in slow motion. About the things that stay with you — not because you chose them, but because they chose you.

Necessary Cuts is the winner of the 2026 Miami University Press Novella Prize and will be published by Miami University Press.
